What are Elemental Clones?

Elemental clones are chakra constructs that inherit traits of an element. Unlike shadow clones (which are fully solid and transfer memories), elemental clones typically have a themed on‑contact effect and different durability/cost profiles. This page gives a quick, game‑ready breakdown.

Lore-friendly; not a 1:1 canon mapping. Use as a design baseline.

Design Notes

  • Cost is how much chakra a single clone drains relative to others.
  • Durability is the rough hit tolerance before the clone breaks.
  • Contact Effect is a status or burst applied when a target touches the clone.
  • Environment toggles modify stats (e.g., Wet boosts Water; Stormy boosts Lightning).
